Fostering crucial conversations on race and racism

The Black Voices at UC San Diego initiative strives to foster a welcoming, understanding, and well-informed environment for prospective and current Black students. We want to combat bigotry and ignorance by supplying information and creating opportunities for open dialogue within our institution.

About BVatUCSD

Who are we?

Black Voices at UC San Diego aims to be a resource on historical and contemporary issues that impact Black students. We strive to provide access to information on allyship, spotlight opportunities and offer a safe space for Black students, and create a place to have open conversations on race and racism.
